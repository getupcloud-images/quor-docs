# Accounts and Plans

Every Quor account can subscribe to images from the catalog, as long as it is linked to an active plan. Currently, Quor offers two types of plans:

## Trial (14 days free)

- Integrated vulnerability scanner
- Automatic rebuilds
- SBOM and provenance included
- Subscription to up to 2 pre-selected images

## Enterprise

- Complete catalog of hardened images (application, infrastructure, and observability)
- Daily builds and continuous updates
- SLA of up to 7 days for critical vulnerability fixes
- Enterprise support with assisted implementation
- Auditable signatures and provenance
- Centralized management of teams, users, and permissions

For more information about plans and how to choose the best fit for your company, contact our sales team.
