# Image Subscriptions

Quor is a container image catalog, and its usage model is based on image subscriptions.

When you subscribe to an image from the catalog, you unlock access to pull any version and any tag associated with that image.

To subscribe, you need an active plan, currently available as Trial or Enterprise.

# Managing Subscribed Images

In the Quor dashboard, under `Settings` > `Organization`, you can view all images your organization has subscribed to.

On this page you can also track:

- Subscription date and user;
- Active plan and validity period.

To access these details, you need a valid account and active plan: [Quor](https://app.quor.dev/settings)
